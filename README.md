# DITHERVOID // PANDORA

**Generative glitch loop engine.** One HTML file. Zero dependencies. Tap the box.

**▶ LIVE: https://0xgreencross.github.io/PANDORA/**

Every tap mints a seamless 1080×1080 animated GIF loop that has never existed and will
never exist again — unless you hold its DNA card.

## What's inside the box

- **37 corruption sigils** across five tiers — analog decay, heavy destruction, oblivion,
  valhalla, demiurge — including a feedback TUNNEL, KALEIDOMANCY, phosphor BLOOM,
  EMBER swarms, RUNE marquees, and CHIMERA: a per-seed *compiled* effect assembled
  from an operation grammar, unique to every seed.
- **8 signal sources** — plasma, ring interference, glyph fields, oscilloscope scans,
  circuit lattices, orbital bodies, geological strata, radial tendrils.
- **∞ colorways** — 14 fixed fluorescent palettes plus GENESIS: procedural neon
  colorways synthesized from the seed. White is doctrinally impossible in output.
- **Per-seed alphabets** — every seed mints its own 10–14 character script from one of
  five glyph grammar families.
- **DNA cards** — `PNDR-XXXX-…` codes carrying the complete genome: seed, colorway,
  source, corruption, frames, every sigil state. CRC-guarded. Byte-exact resurrection
  of any loop, on any device, forever.
- **Anti-similarity mint** — each tap auditions 24 candidate universes and keeps the one
  maximally distant from everything recently generated.
- **𝕏-safe export** — a parallel-encoded twin guaranteed under the 15MB GIF cap.
- Native GIF89a/LZW encoder, mathematically seamless loops, deterministic renders,
  25fps, flat frames, no white, no gaps.

## Use

Open the page. **Tap the monitor** to open the box — tap again any time, even
mid-render, to abandon and re-mint. `TRANSMIT` renders your dialed settings.
`☠` mints at corruption 100. `⤓ SAVE` / `𝕏` export. The `LAB` tab holds the seed
and DNA card tools.

## Provenance

A DNA card **is** the artwork. Same card, same pixels, byte-identical SHA-256 —
verified. Cards are versioned; v1 and v2 cards both decode.
